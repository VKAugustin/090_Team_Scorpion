Election Promises and Policies

Political Achievements

  • Infrastructure Projects: Spearheaded regional urban development initiatives during his time as Minister of Regional Development, focusing on infrastructure improvements and modernization efforts.
  • Wildlife and Environmental Protection: As the Minister of Wildlife and Sustainable Development, implemented measures to enhance wildlife conservation and sustainable development practices across Sri Lanka.
  • National Security Advocacy: Advocated for military reforms and professionalization of the armed forces, using his extensive military experience to push for stronger national security policies.
  • Restoration of Civic Rights: Played a key role in restoring his civic rights in 2015, marking a significant comeback in politics and representing the United National Party (UNP) as a National List MP.
  • Presidential Candidate: Contested the 2010 presidential election as the main opposition candidate, contributing to the political discourse on governance and national security in Sri Lanka.
