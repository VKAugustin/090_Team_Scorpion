Profile of Sajith Premadasa

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Sajith Premadasa
  • Date of Birth: January 9, 1967
  • Age: 57
  • Nationality: Sri Lankan
  • Education: Royal College, Colombo; University of Exeter (Bachelor of Science in Economics)
  • Profession: Politician
  • Spouse: Sashini Premadasa
  • Symbol: Telephone
  • Political Status: Running as the candidate of the Samagi Jana Balawegaya (SJB) in the 2024 Presidential Election
  • Residency: Colombo, Sri Lanka

Political Background and Career

  • Current Position: Leader of the Samagi Jana Balawegaya (SJB)
  • Past Roles: Minister of Housing and Construction, Minister of Urban Development and Housing
  • Political Party: Samagi Jana Balawegaya (SJB)
  • Parliamentary Service: Member of Parliament for Hambantota District

2024 Presidential Election

  • Election Status: Running as the SJB's candidate
  • Campaign Focus: Emphasizes economic revival, anti-corruption, social welfare, and infrastructure development
  • Election Symbol: Telephone

Election Promises and Policies

  • Economic Revival: Plans to stimulate the economy through investment in infrastructure and job creation
  • Anti-Corruption: Pledges to combat corruption and ensure transparency in governance
  • Social Welfare: Aims to improve healthcare, education, and social services
  • Infrastructure Development: Focuses on modernizing transport and urban infrastructure

Political Achievements

  • Infrastructure Projects: Spearheaded several urban development projects
  • Social Programs: Implemented housing programs and improved public amenities

Controversies and Criticisms

  • Handling of Housing Projects: Criticized for inefficiencies and delays in some housing projects
  • Political Rivalries: Faced opposition from rival political factions within the SJB

Personal and Family Life

  • Family: Comes from a prominent political family; son of former President R. Premadasa
  • Spouse's Involvement: Sashini Premadasa is known for her charitable work and social initiatives

Visual Representation

  • Appearance: Known for his formal attire and professional demeanor
  • Election Symbol: Telephone
