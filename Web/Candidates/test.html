Ranil Wickremesinghe

Independent Candidate for Sri Lanka's 2024 Presidential Election

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Ranil Wickremesinghe
  • Date of Birth: March 24, 1949 (Age 75)
  • Nationality: Sri Lankan
  • Education: Royal College, Colombo; University of Ceylon (Bachelor of Laws); Ceylon Law College
  • Profession: Attorney at Law
  • Spouse: Maithree Wickremesinghe (Professor at University of Kelaniya)

Political Status

  • Current Position: President of Sri Lanka (since July 21, 2022)
  • Prime Minister: Served six terms (1993–1994, 2001–2004, 2015–2018, 2018–2019, 2022)
  • Running as: Independent candidate in 2024 Presidential Election

2024 Presidential Election

  • Election Focus: Economic recovery, national unity, good governance, social development
  • Campaign Symbol: Yet to be declared
  • Support Base: Expected to attract cross-party support
  • Campaign Promises:
    • Economic Recovery: Comprehensive reforms to address ongoing crisis
    • Good Governance: Transparency, anti-corruption, institutional reform
    • National Unity: Reconciliation among Sri Lanka’s diverse ethnic and religious groups
    • Social Welfare: Improved healthcare, education, and social services

Political Achievements

  • Economic Liberalization: Advocated for international trade and market liberalization
  • Institutional Reforms: Strengthened democratic institutions and governance
  • Infrastructure Development: Supported development of economic zones and infrastructure projects

Controversies & Criticisms

  • Batalanda Allegations: Accusations of human rights abuses in the late 1980s (no criminal charges)
  • 2018 Constitutional Crisis: Controversial removal and reinstatement as Prime Minister
  • Handling of Economic Crisis: Mixed reactions to his economic policies during presidency

Personal & Family Life

  • Family: Comes from a politically influential family in Sri Lanka
  • Spouse: Maithree Wickremesinghe, academic and advocate for women’s rights
